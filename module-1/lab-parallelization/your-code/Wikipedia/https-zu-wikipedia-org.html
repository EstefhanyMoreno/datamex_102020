Ihlanganiswa amakhilomitha ayizi-2,798 agudle usebe lolwandle okuyi iNdiyeni kanye ne-Atlanthiki. Amazwe angomakhelwane ahlanganisa iNamibhiya, iBotswana, iZimbabwe, Mozambiki kanye neSwazi; iLesotho iyizwe elincane elizungezwe iNingizimu Afrika. Umongameli waseNingizimu Afrika ngu Mnz.Matamela Cyril Ramaphosa (okuningi). Bewazi Ukuthi... IsiZulu singulimi lwamaZulu. Ngulimi lwesiNguni. Sikhulunywa eNingizimu Afrika ngabantu abayizi-10 677 305 (ukubala kwesizwe kwezi-2001) futhi siqondwa ngabantu abayizi-25000000.
