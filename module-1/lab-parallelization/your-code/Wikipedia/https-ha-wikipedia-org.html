Mukaloli 5,467 a cikin harshen Hausa Adabi Tarihin mutane Addini Tarihi Lissafi Kimiyya Zamantakewa Fasaha Wikipedia Muhimmiyar SanarwaBarka da zuwa Hausa Wikipedia, Ɗaya daga cikin shafukan Wikipedia a Harshen Hausa, zaku iya rubutawa da ingantawa ko kuma ƙirƙiran sabbin muƙaloli kamar yadda kuke gani, dan taimako wurin rubuta kundin Ilimi na Insakulofidiya wadda ke taskance ilimi dan masu karatu da yin bincike a harshen Hausa.Ku karanta shafin mu na Gabatarwa.
